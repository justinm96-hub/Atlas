# Atlas Vision

Atlas is an all-in-one business operating system built specifically for real estate photographers.

Its goal is to reduce the amount of time spent managing a business while increasing organization, automation, and consistency.

Atlas should feel modern, fast, and effortless to use.

Core principles:

- Speed over clutter
- One-click actions whenever possible
- Information should be visible immediately
- Every screen should answer "What do I need to do next?"
- Automation should assist, never surprise
